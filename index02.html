About The Boys

Michael and Ming were inseparable brothers found at a local animal shelter. After a short time in foster care, they quickly found their forever home and began their journey as YouTube sensations.

Michael, the larger and more laid-back of the two, enjoys long naps and demanding treats. Ming, the energetic explorer, is known for his daring climbing skills and endless curiosity. Together, they bring chaos and cuddles in equal measure.

Follow their weekly adventures and see why they are the most beloved cat brothers on the internet!
